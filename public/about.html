About Me

After graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), I found myself at a crossroads. Although I had completed my degree, I initially hesitated to pursue a career in the field. Doubts filled my mind, and I felt unsure about my abilities and whether I was truly ready to step into the IT industry. As a result, I took a different path, exploring other opportunities that seemed more accessible and comfortable at the time.

However, as the years went by, something within me began to stir. Deep down, I knew that my true passion lay in technology and the limitless possibilities it held. It was during this period of self-reflection and growth that I discovered a newfound determination to embrace my calling.

One person played a pivotal role in this transformation: my boyfriend. He had followed his own dreams and become a successful software developer, constantly inspiring me with his knowledge, skills, and unwavering passion for his work. Witnessing his achievements and observing the impact he made in the IT industry ignited a fire within me, pushing me to confront my fears and dive headfirst into the world of technology.

With newfound motivation and a burning desire to succeed, I made the firm decision to pursue my BSIT degree and embark on a career in the IT industry. Recognizing the need to bridge the gap between my current knowledge and the evolving demands of the field, I sought out additional learning opportunities to sharpen my skills.

Udemy became my go-to platform for acquiring valuable knowledge and practical expertise. I enrolled in a variety of courses, ranging from programming languages to database management and cybersecurity. Each course provided me with the tools and insights necessary to thrive in the dynamic landscape of IT.

As I delve into the courses on Udemy, I am amazed by the wealth of information available at my fingertips. Expert instructors share their expertise, guiding me through complex concepts and hands-on projects. I eagerly devour the content, eager to build a solid foundation and become proficient in the skills required for a successful career in the IT industry.

Though challenges may arise along the way, I am determined to overcome them with diligence and perseverance. The journey to becoming a skilled IT professional may be daunting, but I am fueled by my passion and the unwavering support of my loved ones.

In the end, my decision to pursue my BSIT degree and prepare myself through Udemy courses represents a personal and professional transformation. I have shed my doubts, embraced my true calling, and embarked on a path that will empower me to make a meaningful impact in the ever-evolving world of technology.
